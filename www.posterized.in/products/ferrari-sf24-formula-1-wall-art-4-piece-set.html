A bold tribute to the passion, speed, and racing heritage of Scuderia Ferrari.

This premium 4-piece split poster features the Ferrari SF-24 from a dramatic top-down perspective, highlighting the engineering precision and iconic design that define Formula 1's most legendary team. The vibrant Rosso Corsa red background, clean geometric composition, and striking vertical typography create a powerful centerpiece for any motorsport-inspired space.

About Ferrari:

Scuderia Ferrari is the most iconic and successful team in Formula 1 history. Since its debut in 1950, Ferrari has become synonymous with speed, innovation, and racing excellence. With countless Grand Prix victories, legendary drivers, and a passionate global fanbase, the Prancing Horse remains one of the most respected names in motorsport.
